Connect Pneumatic Device


Every pneumatic device has different number of ports. When not connected, every port is marked with a red circle.

When user wants to connect pneumatic two devices, he clicks on a port of one device. The circle of the port will disappear. When the mouse is moved, a line is drawn which represents the connection line. Then, he clicks on a port of the other device and complete the connection. During the process, if he wants to give up the connection, just right clicks on the mouse.

If user wants to delete a line, he clicks on the line. The line is chosen and is shown with a wide line. Then, he right clicks on the line to bring up popup windows and clicks on the "delete" function to complete the delete action.
