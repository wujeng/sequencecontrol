Computer-aided Electrical Circuit Design

 

An electrical circuit is formed by electrical device symbols and wires. To design a circuit, user puts device symbols on the circuit panel and connects them. To put a device symbol on the circuit panel, user first move the cursor to an appropriate position by clicking on the position. He then clicks on the symbol in the electrical device windows, an instance of the symbol will be shown in the cursor position.

Block Edit:
User can use mouse to drag out a selected block in the circuit panel. Then, he can operate (move, delete, cut, ...) on the circuit block by using popup windows (see the figure below).
